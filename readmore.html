More About Us

We offer Commitment to Excellence. Quality is the cornerstone of our work. From the selection of premium materials to the careful execution of each design, we are committed to delivering products that reflect the highest standards of craftsmanship. Our dedication to excellence extends beyond the finished piece; it's embedded in the entire experience of working with Scriby Nib. Welcome to Scratchy Nib, where the art of beautiful writing comes to life. We are passionate about the timeless elegance of calligraphy and dedicated to sharing its beauty with the world.At the heart of our studio is a team of dedicated calligraphers. With an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship and a keen eye for detail, we transform ordinary words into extraordinary works of art. Our creations are a testament to the beauty that unfolds when tradition meets contemporary flair.

Our Story

At the heart of Scratchy Nib is a story of passion and creativity. Founded in 2023, our journey began with a deep appreciation for the art of calligraphy. What started as a personal exploration soon evolved into a mission to spread the joy and artistry of handwritten expression.

At Scratchy Nib, we believe that every stroke tells a story and every curve conveys emotion. Our passion for calligraphy goes beyond the ink on paper and it's about creating timeless pieces that capture the essence of the written word. Whether it's a wedding invitation, a meaningful quote, or a custom piece of art, we infuse each creation with a touch of elegance and a dash of personality.
